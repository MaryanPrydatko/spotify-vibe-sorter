What changed from the earlier proposal: these are specific listening patterns, not generic vibe buckets. A song can belong to more than one pattern. The point is to design fewer daily playlists without losing the weird niche stuff.

Most important correction

The electronic side is not one bucket. It splits into hard tekno, IDM/braindance, ambient-liminal, and jazz-house. Your screenshot track, FI3AC2265060 by Aleksi Perala, belongs with the IDM/braindance pattern.

Likely playlist strategy

Keep personal/archive playlists separate. Build daily playlists from specific functional patterns: hard tekno, gym/rage, sad art-rock, Frank/Montell R&B, shower/warm songs, and chill/ambient.
